Make a Gift



Donate

A gift to Arlington Life Shelter will help homeless men, women, and children living in poverty find a path to self-sufficiency. Your gift will support critical life-changing services such as food, shelter, case management, and wrap-around services 24/7/365 for the homeless.

You are also welcome to send a check, cash, or money order to: Arlington Life Shelter, 325 W. Division St., Arlington, TX 76011.

Questions? Email us.
