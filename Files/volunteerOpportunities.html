Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteer
Volunteers are integral to our organization- the support we receive from individuals, groups, and other organizations is crucial in assisting our guests create a path of self-sufficiency. Before completing an application, please read our volunteer requirements and expectations below.




Volunteer Requirements
All volunteers are required to:

  • Must be age 18 or older. This is to protect the privacy, safety, and dignity of our youngest guests. Any volunteer under the age of 18 must have a parent or guardian present at all times. Unsupervised children are not allowed. We recommend that very young children not be brought to the shelter while parents are attempting to volunteer.

  • Complete the volunteer application and have a background check every two years.

  • Attend a volunteer orientation and training.

  • Receive on-the-job training with a seasoned volunteer or staff member.

  • Please Note: No community restitution or court-ordered volunteer work can be accepted.




Ways to Serve:

  • Food Donation Pick Up Helper:
    We need someone that can travel to food establishments in the area to pick up food items and deliver them to the shelter (located at 325 W. Division in Arlington). We use these items to feed the 100 residents who live with us as they work to regain their self-sufficiency. For this position, you must have a TX driver’s license and proof of insurance for your vehicle. Depending on the pick up selected, you may need a vehicle with some storage capacity like a truck or SUV. You may also be required to lift up to 25 lbs.


  • Provide a Meal:
    Grab a team of co-workers, a faith group, or your family and friends and cook a meal for residents. We need Food Team Volunteers to purchase, prepare, and serve meals. We need teams to provide breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Bring a small group of friends, coworkers, or maybe to celebrate a birthday as a way to give back. Email alice.morris@arlingtonlifeshelter.org for meal guidelines and available dates.


  • PALS: Partners of Arlington Life Shelter (PALS) is an auxiliary group that fundraises for the shelter and provides special events such as birthday and holiday parties for the residents.



For more information on how to volunteer, please contact alice.morris@arlingtonlifeshelter.org.
